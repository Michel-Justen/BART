How has BART ridership changed since 2018?

These visualizations show how ridership patterns have changed since 2018, using hourly data on where BART riders started their trips. Following the COVID-19 pandemic, ridership fell sharply across all stations and times of day. There has been a slow recovery since then, but not a full one: in 2019 there were 118.6M annual station origins, while 2025 saw only 54.5M (46% recovery).
